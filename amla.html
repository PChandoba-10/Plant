Amla, also known as Indian Gooseberry (Phyllanthus emblica), is a nutrient-rich fruit renowned for its medicinal properties in Ayurveda.

Uses:

  • Rich in Vitamin C, it boosts immunity and promotes healthy skin and hair.
  • Used in Ayurvedic medicine for digestion, heart health, and diabetes management.
  • Commonly consumed in the form of juice, dried powder, or as an ingredient in herbal supplements.
