🌿 Description:

Snake Plant, also known as Sansevieria or Mother-in-law's Tongue, is a popular indoor plant known for its striking upright leaves and air-purifying benefits.

🍃 Uses:

  • Improves indoor air quality by filtering out toxins and releasing oxygen at night.
  • Requires minimal care, making it ideal for both beginner and busy plant enthusiasts.
  • Often used as an attractive decorative element in homes and offices.
