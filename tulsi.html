🌿 Description:

Tulsi, also known as Holy Basil (Ocimum sanctum), is a revered herb in Indian culture. It is celebrated for its spiritual significance and medicinal properties.

🍃 Uses:

  • Traditionally used in Ayurvedic medicine to relieve stress, treat respiratory issues, and boost immunity.
  • Known for its antioxidant, antimicrobial, and anti-inflammatory properties.
  • Commonly used in herbal teas, supplements, and ritual practices.
