Mango (Mangifera indica) is a tropical fruit tree native to South Asia, celebrated for its delicious fruit and cultural significance.

Uses:

  • Rich in vitamins A and C, it supports immunity, skin health, and vision.
  • Mangoes are consumed fresh, dried, or as juice, jams, and pickles.
  • The leaves and bark are used in traditional medicine for their healing properties.
