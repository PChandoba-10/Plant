🌿 Description:

Neem (Azadirachta indica) is a versatile tree native to the Indian subcontinent, highly valued for its medicinal and pesticidal properties.

🍃 Uses:

  • Used in traditional Ayurvedic medicine for treating skin disorders, infections, and dental issues.
  • Its leaves, bark, and seeds serve as natural insect repellents and components in organic pesticides.
  • Incorporated in herbal remedies, personal care products, and sustainable agriculture practices.
